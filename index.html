About Me

Hello! I'm Paul Ramirez.

I'm a software engineer, photographer, and videographer from NYC. I love bringing ideas and visions to life with technology. My mission is to improve the lives of as many people as I can.

I was previously pursuing a career in physical therapy because I took great pride in directly impacting and taking care of people. However, I decided to make the switch to the tech industry because I knew I would be able to solve greater problems and reach a much wider range of people all over the world.

I recently graduated from App Academy, an intensive coding bootcamp where I learned the skills necessary to thrive as a software engineer. I'm currently seeking a fullstack software engineering position where I can collaborate with other like-minded individuals towards the advancement of our society.

In my free time, I enjoy rocking out on my guitar, powerlifting, basketball, snowboarding, and collecting sneakers.
